Introduction

Subvert

Subvert is a contemporary art gallery dedicated to promoting professional artists seeking exposure to the Los Angeles art Market. Artists arecurrated to fit Subvert's underground and alternative brand.

The target audience for Subvert is art collectors, art enthusiests, and other artists

Art enthusiests looking to view or purchase underground artworks online will find that this site meets thier needs and expectations.

The primary tasks for the viewers of the Subvert site are to view artworks, learn about artists and the Subvert brand/mission, and purchase artworks for their own enjoyment.
